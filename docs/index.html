Welcome to Bathy-Vagues’s documentation!¶

Context¶

This project was made in the context of the “Projet Long” at ENSEEIHT Toulouse during February 2019. In team of 5, we tackled a machine learning open research problem coming from a client needs and tried to propose a solution. We were supervised and evaluated by an industrial actor from Altran (Toulouse) on our professionalism and our ability to work and lead a team project. We are proud to share with you our work and its results in this little internet webpage document.

Description¶

Nowday, litoral regions are facing major environmental issues coming from demographic pressures and overexploitation, it is both dangerous for the sea nature maintenance and the population living there, experimenting in some areas extreme wheather events as flood and erosion. For all these reasons we are interested in this work to improve the wave dynamic representation with non invasive and cheap data such as satellite images. We hope, by leaning on a timestack representation of the waves, to succeed to show the interest of machine learning as a potential and maybe good solution to predict the bathymetry of the beach.
